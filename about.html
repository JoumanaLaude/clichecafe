About Us

Cliche Cafe is a cafe and lounge area where we provide people with an exquisite experience for food, work, or relaxation. Our cafe side includes a seasonal rotating menu for food and drinks inspired by Asian cuisine. We also have a special bubble tea bar where people can enjoy the combination of boba flavors & alcohol. The cafe provides plenty of spaces with tables and seating where people can do work or just kick back and take a breather. These spaces are first-come, first-serve to the public, unless you reserve ahead of time. Our staff works to provide a unique experience to help introduce people to the modern way of life.

Our mission is to provide a learning experience through food, a comfortable and new workspace, and help introduce people to the modern way of life.
